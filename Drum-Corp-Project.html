St Laurence O Toole Pipe Band!

Meet the drum corp!

My name is Aoife Bissett and I am a part of the world championship winning drum corp from St Laurence O Toole! In our drum corp we have 7 tenor drummers and 12 snare drummers. The drum sergeant is Stephen Creighton, and the bass drummer is John Dunne. The 7 tenor drummers are Simon Hodgett, Laura Paynter, Shauna Byrne, Samantha Duffy, Ewan Glenny, Maurethe Leonard and myself.
