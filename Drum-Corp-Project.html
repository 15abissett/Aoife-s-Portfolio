St Laurence O Toole Drum Corp

Meet the drum corp!

My name is Aoife Bissett and I am a part of the world championship winning drum corp from St Laurence O Toole! In our drum corp we have 7 tenor drummers and 12 snare drummers. The tenors are me, Simon Hodgett, Laura Paynter, Shauna Byrne, Samantha Duffy, Ewan Glenny, and Maurethe Leonard. The snare drummers are Conor McNally, David Murphy, Conor Lawlor, Glen Ceighton, Andrew Miller, Stephen Creighton, Robbie Barrett, Mikey McKenna, Grant Cassidy, Chris Lawrie and Craig Lawrie
